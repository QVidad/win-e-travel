Welcome to the Tour Guiding Training App

Tour guiding is more than just leading people from one place to another — it's about bringing destinations to life, sharing meaningful stories, and ensuring travelers have memorable, safe, and enjoyable experiences.

As a tour guide, you are not only the bridge between visitors and culture, but also an ambassador of your community and its heritage.

Your Role as a Tour Guide

Provide accurate and engaging information about the sites, culture, and history.

Ensure the comfort, safety, and enjoyment of your guests.

Represent your destination with professionalism, integrity, and enthusiasm.

Adapt to different group needs and create a welcoming atmosphere for everyone.

After this training, you will gain:

Adaptability
Communication Skills
Crisis Management
Cultural & Historical Knowledge
Customer Service
Empathy
Integrity
Professionalism
Respect

How to Use This Training App

1
Sign In or Create an Account

This allows you to track your progress, scores, and unlocked content.

2
Select a Training Segment

Modules and lessons unlock in sequence. You must pass the current lesson or module before moving on.

3
Learn & Practice

Watch training videos, read lesson guides, and try interactive activities to build your knowledge, skills, and attitudes.

4
Take Quick Checks

Short quizzes appear at the end of each lesson.

Passing requirement: Score 90% or higher to unlock the next lesson.
You may take Quick Checks without a facilitator. If you score below 90%, review the lesson materials and retake until you pass.
5
Complete the End-of-Module Evaluation

A formal 25-item test combining scenario-based and identification questions.

Passing requirement: Score 90% or higher to unlock the next module.
Must be completed in the presence of a facilitator. If you score below 90%, review the module content and retake with the facilitator present.
6
Complete Simulations

Apply your learning in virtual tour scenarios to demonstrate practical skills and decision-making.

7
Advance to the Next Module or Lesson

The system automatically unlocks the next section when you meet the 90% passing score for the relevant Quick Check or End-of-Module Evaluation.

8
Earn Your Certificate

Once all modules are completed, download your official training certificate.

Whether you're preparing for your first guided tour or refining years of experience, this app is your step-by-step companion to becoming a confident, knowledgeable, and inspiring tour guide.
