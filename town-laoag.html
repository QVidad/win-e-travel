Laoag City

Capital of Ilocos Norte • Sunshine City

About Laoag City

The City of Laoag is a 3rd class component city and the capital of the province of Ilocos Norte. It is the province's most populous, political, commercial, and industrial hub and the location of Ilocos Region's busiest commercial airport. It is also the northernmost city in the Philippines.

The name "Laoag" is a derivative of the Ilocano term which means light or brightness. Buzeta, an Augustinian missionary, states that Laoag has a clear atmosphere which presents a beautiful, blue skyline. Laoag is also called the "Sunshine City" of the Philippines.

On June 19, 1965, Laoag changed from municipality to city status. It has a total land area of 116.08 km² subdivided into 80 barangays.

Key Attractions

Sinking Bell Tower

Laoag City is famous for its "Sinking Bell Tower" which sinks into the ground at a rate of an inch a year. The tower, built presumably after the 1707 earthquake, has a foundation of 90 meters and a height of 45 meters. It is made of locally manufactured bricks joined by molasses and juice of sablot leaves mixed with lime and sand.

St. William's Cathedral

Built in 1612 by Augustinian friars to replace a wooden chapel. The church is known for its Italian Renaissance design and unusual two-storey façade. It serves as the seat of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Laoag.

Aurora Park

Situated at the center surrounded by St. William Cathedral, Laoag City Hall, Marcos Hall of Justice, Ilocos Norte Provincial Capitol, and the Sinking Bell Tower. The plaza was originally called Plaza de Alfonso XII after the King of Spain.

Museo Ilocos Norte

Nicknamed "Gameng" meaning treasure in Iloco. Erected in 1878 as the Tabacalera Building, it served as the Administrative Center of the Tobacco Monopoly. Now it's a repository of the vast cultural heritage of Ilocos Norte.

La Paz Sand Dunes

A popular geological site spanning about 88 square kilometers. Known for 4x4 jeep rides and sandboarding activities. Filming location for local and international movies.
